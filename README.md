# Explodotech

## What is this about?
This is supposed to be a game about spaceships. Spaceships and missiles and sensors.
Ships to fly around in, sensors to see the enemy and missiles to shoot at them. 

But what do you do if YOU are the one being shot at? Well if it's a projectile you just step out of the way... if it's a powered missile you may try to outrun it... 
But how do you know do you know what is coming at you? That'S where your sensors come back in...

There is a lot still to do. Anyone is welcome to download the code and give it a try. Suggestions are also always welcome.
